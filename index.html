CURRENT PROJECT WHAT I'M WORKING ON AT THE MOMENT

FINAL YEAR PROJECT XR INTERACTION DESIGN

I'm currently completing my final year project as part of my degree in Product Design and Technology in the University of Limerick. My thesis is an exploration into the ways that people might be better able to interact with XR interfaces and environments, and acted as a platform for me to start prototyping interactions around holistic use case scenarios.

The project began with the development of a discrete wearable input device that would facilitate direct interactions with XR UIs and provide multimodal feedback to users, but as the project developed I realised that whatever input device is being used for XR needs to facilitate both direct and indirect interactions with digital content.

As a result the project has moved towards an investigation into the structure of an XR operating system and the interaction patterns that might facilitate more natural and efficient content manipulation and navigation.
